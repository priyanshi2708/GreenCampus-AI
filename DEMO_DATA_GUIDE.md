# GreenCampus AI — Presentation & Demonstration Guide

Welcome to the GreenCampus AI Presentation Sandbox! This guide is designed to help you demonstrate the platform to professors, investors, colleagues, or judges in a simple, cohesive, and professional way.

---

## 🌟 The Demo Campus: Evergreen State University

When you seed the demo dataset, the system configures **Evergreen State University** as your presentation sandbox, generating the following rich, realistic information:

*   **7 Core Buildings & Departments**:
    *   **Main Academic Block (MAB)**: Computer Science, Mathematics, Physics
    *   **Science & Tech Hub (STH)**: Chemistry, Biotechnology, Research Lab
    *   **Student Union (STU)**: Events & Culture, Canteen, Recreation
    *   **Library Complex (LBC)**: Digital Resources, Archives, Reading Halls
    *   **Admin Building (ADB)**: HR & Finance, Management, IT Services
    *   **Sports Facility (SPF)**: Athletics, Aquatics, Gymnasium
    *   **Engineering Block (ENB)**: Mechanical, Civil, Electrical
*   **30 Days of Daily Resource Logs**: Over 200 resource readings dynamically generated with realistic weekday/weekend oscillation curves.
*   **Pre-Calculated ML Predictions**: Beautifully formatted forecasts for Electricity, Water, Waste, and Carbon metrics (no need to wait for Python ML scripts).
*   **3 Real-World Anomalies (Alerts)**:
    1.  *Critical (Open)*: HVAC Chiller Efficiency Deficit in Science & Tech Hub (Energy)
    2.  *Warning (Investigating)*: Restroom Flush Valve Leakage in Library Complex (Water)
    3.  *Info (Resolved)*: Zero Single-Use Plastic Program Milestone in Student Union (Waste)
*   **Student & Department Leaderboards**: Complete profile standings pitting your admin profile against competitive mock students (Sophia, Liam, Olivia) and departments.

---

## 🚀 How to Seed the Demo Data

1.  Log in to your admin account (or register a new user).
2.  Navigate to the **Settings** page (accessed from the sidebar or the profile dropdown in the top bar).
3.  Scroll down to the **Sandbox & Presentation Mode** section.
4.  Click **Seed Presentation Demo Data**.
5.  Wait a moment for the success notification. The page will auto-reload, and your entire dashboard will be fully populated with beautiful, interactive datasets!

## 🎤 Interactive Presentation Walkthrough Script

Here is a step-by-step guide to structure your presentation:

### Step 1: The Hook — The Campus Executive Dashboard
*   **Where**: Home Dashboard (`/dashboard`)
*   **What to say**: 
    > *"Welcome to GreenCampus AI, a premium SaaS platform transforming universities into smart, net-zero communities. Upon logging in, campus executives are met with a unified command center. Here, we see our real-time AI Sustainability Score (86/100), key resource performance indicators (KPIs), and historical trend charts showing resource consumption across the entire university."*
*   **What to show**: Point out the **AI Sustainability Score**, **Metrics Grid**, and **Daily Trends Chart**. Point out the **Onboarding & Setup Checklist** which now shows 100% completion status.

### Step 2: The Action — Granular Resource Tracking
*   **Where**: Resource Tracking Center (`/dashboard/resources`)
*   **What to say**:
    > *"Next, we look at where the data comes from. Administrators can integrate IoT smart meters or log daily utility readings manually. Our resource tracking covers Electricity (kWh), Water (Liters), and Waste (kg). The system automatically converts electricity and waste into carbon footprint equivalents in real time."*
*   **What to show**: 
    1. Scroll down to show the **Resource History Table** containing the 30-day daily logs.
    2. Click **Add Entry** to show the popup form. Log a quick mock entry (e.g., Building: *Engineering Block*, Department: *Mechanical*, Electricity: *450*, Water: *1200*, Waste: *30*) and click submit to show the instant update.
    3. Use the filter dropdowns to display only "Library Complex" to demonstrate fast database querying.

### Step 3: The Intelligence — Forecasting & Simulation
*   **Where**: AI Predictions Page (`/dashboard/predictions`)
*   **What to say**:
    > *"Instead of just looking backwards, we use machine learning to look forwards. Our forecasting engine trains on historical data to predict resource demands for the next 30 days. Furthermore, we built a Scenario Simulator allowing managers to test sustainability initiatives before spending capital."*
*   **What to show**:
    1. Highlight the **30-Day Forecast Charts** showing predictions with confidence intervals.
    2. Scroll down to the **Scenario Simulator**. Move the sliders (e.g., increase Solar Energy to 40% and convert Lighting to 100% LED) and show how the projected carbon emissions and cost savings adjust dynamically.

### Step 4: The Diagnostics — The AI Alert Center
*   **Where**: Alert Center (`/dashboard/alerts`)
*   **What to say**:
    > *"When resource spikes occur, our anomaly detection algorithms trigger alerts immediately. Here, we see a critical alert for the Science & Tech Hub, where electricity consumption surged by 42%. The system doesn't just flag the issue; it diagnoses potential causes and recommends actions."*
*   **What to show**:
    1. Click on the critical alert **"HVAC Chiller Efficiency Deficit"** to expand the root-cause diagnostics.
    2. Highlight the **Root Cause Details**: What Happened, Why Flagged (Z-Score), Potential Cause, and Recommended Action. This showcases true "AI-driven actionability."

### Step 5: The Community — Gamification & Behavior Change
*   **Where**: Campus Challenges & Leaderboard (`/dashboard/gamification`)
*   **What to say**:
    > *"Technology alone isn't enough; we need cultural shift. GreenCampus gamifies sustainability by engaging students and departments. Users join challenges—like the LED Conversion Sprint—and earn points to unlock badges and climb the leaderboards."*
*   **What to show**:
    1. Show the **unlocked badges** (Green Novice, Eco Champion, Carbon Slayer) and **points streak**.
    2. Point out the **Student Leaderboard** showing Sophia, Liam, Olivia, and your account.
    3. Point out the **Department Leaderboard** where the *School of Engineering* is currently leading.

### Step 6: The Executive Report — Certified ESG PDF Downloads
*   **Where**: Sustainability Reports Center (`/dashboard/reports`)
*   **What to say**:
    > *"Finally, we offer executive compliance reporting. With one click, administrators can generate certified Sustainability and ESG Performance PDF reports complete with charts, anomaly statistics, and carbon reductions, ready to present to school boards or auditors."*
*   **What to show**:
    1. Click **Generate Report** or view the pre-seeded reports list.
    2. Click **Download PDF** on an existing report to show the instant creation/generation of clean documents.
